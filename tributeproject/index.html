Steven Paul Jobs

The man who changed the world

Here's a timeline of notable events in Steve Job's life:

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, CA
  • 1968 - Obtains a summer job at Hewlett Packard (HP) after calling HP's CEO Bill Hewlett, looking for spare parts for a project
  • 1970 - Meets Steve Wozinak
  • 1972 - Enrolls at Reed College only to drop out a semeseter later
  • 1974 - Obtains a job at Atari as an engineer
  • 1976 - Cofounds Apple Computer with Steve Wozinak and Ronald Wayne
  • 1977 - Apple releases the Apple II, it's first successful computer
  • 1984 - Apple releases the Macintosh through a spectacular presentation by Jobs, even having Macintosh speak for itself
  • 1985 - Leaves Apple after being ousted by then CEO John Sculley
  • 1986 - Buys what would become Pixar from Lucasfilm
  • 1988 - NEXT Computer (which Jobs founded after being ousted from Apple) releases its first computer
  • 1996 - Returns to Apple as an advisor after Apple buys NEXT
  • 1998 - Apple releases the iMac - the first in a line of successful products still being made today
  • 2000 - Officially becomes CEO of Apple
  • 2001 - Apple releases the iPod
  • 2007 - Apple releases the iPhone
  • 2010 - Apple releases the iPad
  • 2011 - After dealing with pancreatic cancer since 2004, Jobs passes away at 56 years old
